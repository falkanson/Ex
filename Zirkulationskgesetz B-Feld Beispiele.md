r>R \quad \int \vec{B}ds=|\vec{B}(r)|*2\pi r =\mu_{0}I_{\text{innen}}\implies B(r)=\frac{\mu_{0}I}{2\pi r}

r<R \quad B(r)=\frac{\mu |\vec{j}|}{2}r \quad\text{mit}\quad \vec{j}=\frac{I}{2R²}
